Solar → electricity → H₂ → NH₃. A simplified overview of the SSCC flow.

1) Solar → Electricity

PV ~20–23% efficiency.

2) Electrolysis → H₂

≈50–55 kWh per kg H₂ (LHV).

3) NH₃ synthesis

H₂ + N₂ → NH₃ — energy carrier & CCU medium.

4) CCU

NH₃ enables CO₂ capture & utilization.
