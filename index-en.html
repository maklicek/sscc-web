Industrial pilot initiative

SSCC: turning harmful into useful

Capture CO2 from stacks, combine it with H2 from renewables, and convert it to NH3 — a fuel and fertilizer. Cleaner air, lower carbon costs, technology over shutdowns.

An integrated system of PV (with storage), CCUS, and electrolysis that locally reduces emissions by >90% while producing a useful product. Goal: keep skilled industry in the region.
