Pilot industrial project

SSCC: turning harmful into useful

Capture CO2 from stacks, combine with H2 from renewables and convert to NH3 — a fuel and fertilizer. Cleaner air, lower emission costs, technology over shutdowns.

An integrated system of photovoltaics (with storage), CCUS and electrolysis to reduce local emissions by >90% and create a useful product. Goal: retain skilled manufacturing in the region.
