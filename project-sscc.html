SSCC Project

Decentralized synthesis of green NH₃ for CO₂ capture and utilization. Pilot 2 kW → 1.5 kg NH₃/day, target efficiency > 80 %, CO₂ capture > 90 %.

Overview

The goal is to validate a pilot line for NH₃ production and CCU in decentralized settings with a focus on safety, energy balance and economics.
