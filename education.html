Solar → electricity → H₂ → NH₃. A simplified explanation of the SSCC process.

1) Solar → Electricity

Photovoltaics with ~20–23 % efficiency.

2) Electrolysis → H₂

≈50–55 kWh per kg of H₂ (LHV).

3) Synthesis of NH₃

H₂ + N₂ → NH₃ – a carbon-free energy carrier for CCU use.

4) CCU

Ammonia enables capture and reuse of CO₂ in industrial systems.
