Solar → electricity → H₂ → NH₃. A simplified overview of the SSCC flow.

1) Solar → Electricity

Photovoltaics ~20–23% efficiency; output depends on irradiance.

2) Electrolysis → H₂

≈50–55 kWh per kg H₂ (LHV) depending on stack & balance-of-plant.

3) NH₃ synthesis

H₂ + N₂ → NH₃ — carbon-free energy carrier & CCU medium.

4) CCU

NH₃ enables CO₂ capture & utilization in decentralized use-cases.
